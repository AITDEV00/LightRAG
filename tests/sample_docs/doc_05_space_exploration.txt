Space exploration has captured human imagination for centuries. From the first satellite Sputnik in 1957 to the lunar landings and Mars rovers, humanity continues to push the boundaries of what's possible.

Recent achievements and future plans:
- SpaceX's reusable rockets have dramatically reduced launch costs
- NASA's Artemis program aims to return humans to the Moon
- Mars colonization plans are being developed by multiple agencies
- James Webb Space Telescope is revealing distant galaxies

Key organizations in space exploration:
- NASA (United States)
- ESA (European Space Agency)
- CNSA (China National Space Administration)
- Private companies: SpaceX, Blue Origin, Virgin Galactic

The search for extraterrestrial life and the goal of making humanity a multi-planetary species drive continued investment in space technology.
